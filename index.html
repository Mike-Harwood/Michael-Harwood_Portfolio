ABOUT

Who I am and why I design and develop

Incorporating technology into my designs, I strive to infuse my personality, interests, and passions in life, such as music and art. Renowned designers like David Carson and Sean Kernick greatly inspire me, as their design styles reflect their own identities and convey meaningful messages. Utilizing a wide range of media styles, including pencil, ink, photography, computer graphics, and software development, I express my creativity.

Looking ahead, I aspire to enhance my artistic style, broaden my knowledge in software development, and reach my full potential. My goal is to become a skilled communicator through design and technology. I strongly believe that this is just the beginning of an exhilarating lifelong career where my passion for graphic design, technology, and software development intertwine.
